Single Grider Crane

We Manufacture Single girder cranes cost less in many ways as only cross girders are needed; quicker installation; runway beams are lighter enough due to reduced crane dead weight. Single Girder Overhead Cranes in SWLs ranging from 250kg to 100MT in different configurations. Typically, beam or box type main girders are used depending on the span of the crane. Span, Lifting Height & Speed as per customer specifications. We specialize in designing a robust structure for smooth operation. They offer easy access in all the Six directions – Long Travel, Cross Travel & Up/Down movement. Cranes are designed & manufactured in accordance with IS: 3177, IS: 4137 & IS: 807.
Single Girder cranes can be controlled through Independent travelling Pendant Push Button or by Radio Remote Control or from Crane Cabin which which allow operators to stand on the safe side and operate. Operators are in such a way of a safer side to have an obstacle less view for smooth functioning of operations for safe, efficient lifting & shifting of heavy material load in factories, foundry, machine shop, engineering, workshops, warehouse etc. Precise control of all motions is achieved through Variable Frequency Drives. These cranes are built to be capable of withstanding extreme requirements of operating speeds and operating conditions.
