About

We manufacture, Electric Wire Rope Hoist, EOT Cranes, Gantry Cranes & JIB Cranes. We have dedicated ourselves to achieve the ultimate excellence in our products and services. We manufacture Wire Rope Hoist as per IS 807, 3177, 3938 and 4137 standards which assure the quality of products. We are in a position to manufacture various type of Hoists & EOT Cranes as per customer’s requirements.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple. We’re leveraging systems and technology in order to streamline the process of purchasing lifting equipment. The faster and easier you can order the equipment you need, the faster you can meet the demands of your customers. We’re not satisfied with “good enough.” We truly aim to elevate standards in lifting equipment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the top distributor of hoists and cranes. We will make this happen with technology systems and people systems. Our team is the cornerstone of our success and we’re eager to put our team to work for you and yours.
